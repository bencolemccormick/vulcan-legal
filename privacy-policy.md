# Privacy Policy for Vulcan

_Last updated: June 15, 2026_

This Privacy Policy describes how Vulcan ("we," "us," or "the app") handles
information when you use the app. Vulcan is currently in a private testing
phase distributed through Apple TestFlight.

## Information We Collect

**Account information.** When you create an account, we collect your email
address through our authentication provider, Clerk. This is used to create
and secure your account, sign you in, and send account-related messages such
as one-time verification codes.

**App activity.** We store the activity you generate inside the app, such as
the simulated trades ("parlays") you place and your in-app balance. This data
is associated with your account so that your activity is available to you
across sessions and devices.

## How We Use Information

We use the information described above to:

- Create, secure, and manage your account.
- Provide the core functionality of the app, including placing simulated
  trades and tracking your in-app balance.
- Send you account-related communications such as verification codes.

We do **not** sell your information, and we do **not** use it for advertising
or cross-app tracking.

## No Real-Money Gambling

Vulcan is an entertainment app that uses a simulated, in-app balance with no
monetary value. It does not facilitate real-money wagering, deposits, or
withdrawals.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the app:

- **Clerk** — authentication and account management. Clerk processes your
  email address to provide sign-in functionality. See Clerk's privacy policy
  at https://clerk.com/legal/privacy.

These providers process data on our behalf solely to deliver the app's
functionality.

## Data Retention

We retain your account information and app activity for as long as your
account exists. If you would like your account and associated data deleted,
contact us at the email address below and we will remove it.

## Children's Privacy

Vulcan is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect
information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be
reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
